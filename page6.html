ABOUT US

From Crunch to Chill in Every Bite & Sip!

Welcome to FLOATATO CORNEAR, your go-to spot for delicious potato snacks and refreshing float drinks! We specialize in serving crispy fries, flavorful mojos, and fun potato twists—paired perfectly with our signature float drinks like Coke Float, Coffee Float, and Choco Float.
Whether you’re craving a quick snack, hanging out with friends, or just cooling down, we’ve got something for you!
